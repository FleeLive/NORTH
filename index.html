About NØRTH Live Studios

NØRTH Live Studios is a Calgary-based live-production media company specializing in real-time broadcast experiences, multi-feed operations, and immersive storytelling. Our mission is to deliver live productions that feel immediate, authentic, and technically uncompromising.

Rooted in the ethos of Canada's True North, we combine broadcast professionalism with cutting-edge technology to produce high-quality live content across multiple platforms.
